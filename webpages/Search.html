Title


Above the horizontal line, there is a quiet and brilliant sunset. It uses thousands of light arrows to roar through the red clouds of the day, and the clouds burn. At the foot of the setting sun is the sparkling sea, which gradually turns up and is dyed red. This is the last and bloody passion of the sun.
